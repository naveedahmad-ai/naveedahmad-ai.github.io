About Me

Researcher focused on AI, rehabilitation robotics, and applied machine learning

Research Focus

My work spans deep learning for control, intelligent human–robot interaction, and trustworthy AI systems. I'm currently affiliated with the University of Canberra, collaborating with clinicians and engineers to translate research into practical benefit.

Research Interests

Rehabilitation robotics, energy-aware control, deep learning, computer vision, and quantum-inspired computation. My approach combines theoretical rigor with practical applications in healthcare technology.
