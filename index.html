About Me

I am a researcher focusing on AI, rehabilitation robotics, and applied machine learning. My work spans deep learning for control, intelligent human–robot interaction, and trustworthy AI systems. I am currently affiliated with the University of Canberra.

Interests: rehabilitation robotics, energy-aware control, deep learning, computer vision, and quantum-inspired computation. I collaborate with clinicians and engineers to translate research into practical benefit.
